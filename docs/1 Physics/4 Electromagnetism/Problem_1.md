### **1. Lorentz Force**

$$
\vec{F} = q(\vec{E} + \vec{v} \times \vec{B})
$$

* $\vec{F}$: Force on the particle (Newtons, N)

* $\vec{v}$: Velocity of the particle (meters/second, m/s)
* $\vec{B}$: Magnetic field (Tesla, T)
